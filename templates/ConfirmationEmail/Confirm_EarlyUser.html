At tracklist.me we believe that the artists should get 100% of the royalties generated by the sales of their music. We do also believe that each artist should get the right amount of money they deserve from music streaming.
With less middlemen, music costs less for you and is more remunerative for the producers.

We do also think that artists should connect more with their fans. That's why we give you the opportunity to get all the useful information about your favorite artists, as well as special discount, limited edition material, and invitation to secret events.

 
It's not an easy mission, but we are working on it.
